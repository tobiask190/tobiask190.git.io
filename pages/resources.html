Usually, when hiking in our two usual locations, heavy hiking gear is not required. Here's a list of what you should bring:

  • Longsleeves, longpants, and longsocks.
  • Good hiking boots
  • A hat with good shading
  • Sunscreen
  • Reusable Bottle
  • Bug Spray

For longer hikes, please visit this site for a more comprehsive list of gear.
Before going on any hike, please visit the California Parks and Recreation site for hiking safety.
